Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

To provide free, fast, and flexible Latin learning.

When I first attempted to learn Latin, I was frustrated by the lack of quality and free online Latin learning sites and programs.
Some programs were behind a paywall costing tens, if not hundreds of dollars, with no guarantee of quality.
Others were free but extremely limited and not tailored for efficient or practical learning.

For too long, methods of learning Latin have been outdated, and through LatinU, I hope to provide a much needed update
to these learning pathways and to increase accessibility and interest in the Latin language.
